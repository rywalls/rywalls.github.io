About me

I’m a curious and creative individual based in Southampton, UK. I love building things.

I spent the first 7 years of my career working in the digital team at independant creative agency Five by Five, where I specialised in producing digital assets for a range of clients.

I like to bring digital assets to life with JavaScript, using libraries such as GSAP and Lottie to add animation.

While most of my professional work falls within the digital arena, I'm also passionate about print and photography.

You can find a couple of my illustrations published in Play For A Living. I have created various books and wedding stationery for family and friends over the years.

I love a good coffee table book or magazine. I also love to make and drink coffee.

I love music. A few of my favourite albums are Trench, KIWANUKA, and Now, Not Yet.
