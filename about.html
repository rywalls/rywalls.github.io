About me

I’m a curious and creative individual based in Southampton, UK. I love building things.

I spent the first 7 years of my career working in the digital team at independant creative agency Five by Five, where I specialised in producing digital assets for a range of clients.

I like to bring digital assets to life with JavaScript, using libraries such as GSAP and Lottie to add animation.
