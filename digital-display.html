I have extensive experience creating standard and rich media banners for leading ad platforms such as DoubleClick (Studio), Sizmek, Google Display Network and Celtra. I use HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and libraries such as GSAP and Lottie to add animation.

Large-scale global campaigns, such as those for the launch of new Call of Duty® titles, require creating the ads in many different sizes and translating them across 25+ languages.

Social

Takeovers + Custom Placements
