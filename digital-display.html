Digital Display

A collection of digital assets—both animated and static—I have created for various brands during my time in a creative agency.

Banner Ads

I have extensive experience creating standard and rich media banners for leading ad platforms such as DoubleClick (Studio), Sizmek, Google Display Network and Celtra. I use HTML, CSS and JavaScript and use libraries such as GSAP and Lottie to add animation.

Large-scale global campaigns, such as those for the launch of new Call of Duty® titles, require creating the ads in many different sizes and translating them across 25+ languages.

Social

I design static social ads for Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter and more, taking into account the various safe zones and ad specs to make sure every ad looks great whichever platform it's shown on.

Takeovers + Custom Placements

I have produced many static and animated homepage takeovers for sites like IGN, Eurogamer, Twitch and Sky Sports, and custom units for things like LG and Samsumg TV menus.
